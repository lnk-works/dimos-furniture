About Us

Welcome to Dimos Furniture, a trusted and leading furniture vendor in Kerala. With our alluring and uniquely designed furniture, we offer a wide range of options that are sure to impress. From renowned brands to our own meticulously crafted ethnic furniture, we showcase our exceptional talent and commitment to quality. Established in 2012 as a retail store in Kollam, we have quickly become the first choice for our valued customers, thanks to our dedication to manufacturing top-notch, customized furniture. Whether you're looking to enhance your home, office, or any interior space, Dimos Furniture is here to transform your surroundings with elegance and style.





Vision

Our vision at Dimos Furniture is to be the proactive leader in the industry, setting new standards of excellence. We aspire to be at the forefront of innovation, offering our customers the most stylish and high-quality furniture solutions. By continuously anticipating and meeting the evolving needs of our customers, we aim to shape the industry and inspire the market with our forward-thinking approach. Our goal is to be recognized as the go-to brand for exceptional designs, unmatched quality, and exceptional customer experiences.




Mission

At Dimos Furniture, our mission is to uplift customers' lifestyles by providing worthy products and services. We are dedicated to offering a wide selection of high-quality furniture at affordable prices, ensuring that our customers can create their dream homes without compromising on quality or budget. With a focus on customer satisfaction and value creation, we aim to be the preferred choice for individuals seeking exceptional furniture solutions.

Why Dimos into Interior designs?

DImos furniture

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space. An interior designer is someone who plans, researches, coordinates, and manages such enhancement projects. Interior design is a multifaceted profession that includes conceptual development, space planning, site inspections, programming and research, communicating with the stakeholders of a project, construction management, and execution of the design. ​

The process of interior design is not always a simple one. Each unique interior design project begins with an initial brief, accompanied by a variety of important aspects, which include client considerations, interior design foundations and project goals, to name a few. Not as simple as purely having an eye for design, although that plays a big part, the interior design process includes planning, analytical thinking and project management. Without this systematic approach used by interior designers across the globe, there would be a lot of unhappy clients and a lot of badly designed interiors.
